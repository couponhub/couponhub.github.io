Ahrefs Plans and Pricing: Straightforward but Premium

Ahrefs uses a credit-based system on lower plans, charging for heavy usage like reports and exports. Annual billing saves ~20% across the board—no Ahrefs promo code needed.

  • Starter → $29/month – 100 credits, basic access; great entry beyond free tools.
  • Lite → $129/month ($108 annual) – Core features, suitable for individuals.
  • Standard → $249/month ($208 annual) – More credits, historical data.
  • Advanced → $449/month ($374 annual) – High limits for teams.
  • Enterprise → $1,499/month (annual commitment) – Custom for big agencies.

Add-ons like extra users or Project Boosts available. No free trial beyond limited free tools, but a money-back guarantee applies.

Core Ahrefs SEO Tools: Where It Excels

Ahrefs packs five main tools, plus extras—focused on data-rich SEO.

Site Explorer: Competitor and Site Analysis

Deep dives into any domain/URL: Organic traffic, top pages, backlinks (35T index), referring domains. Historical data up to years on higher plans.

Keywords Explorer: Precision Research

28.7B keywords, difficulty scores, search volume, clicks data. Filters for questions, low-competition gems—my go-to for content ideas.

Content Explorer: Topic and Gap Hunting

Search billions of pages for trending content, filter by traffic/backlinks. Great for broken link building or replication.

Rank Tracker: Position Monitoring

Daily updates, multi-device/location tracking, visibility scores.

Site Audit: Technical Fixes

Crawls your site, flags 170+ issues, with fixes and prioritization.

New in 2026: Brand Radar for AI visibility (mentions in ChatGPT, Perplexity), SERP feature tracking.
